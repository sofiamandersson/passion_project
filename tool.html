Hey, I'm here to give you a few tips and tricks if you get stuck

1 Check in

Ok, so the first step is to check everyone in, the purpose is to align the team and to set the context of the session. There are no wrong answers!

  1. Gather everyone standing up in a circle
  2. Ask the check-in question; “What’s your expectation of this session?”
  3. One by one answer the question
  4. When everyone has answered you're officially checked-in

2 Why and Background

When giving feedback it’s important to base it of common past experiences within your team, so now its time to go back

  1. Draw a timeline for the past 3 months
  2. Put up post-its on the major events that has gone down for this period and place them on the timeline

4Reflections and Insights

Now when you have both received and given feedback to all team members it’s likely that some unexpected feelings and events took place, which is great

  1. Get a paper and pen
  2. Take a few minutes for individual reflection
  3. Write down your main insights from the session, answering questions like
  4. When everyone gather the team sitting in a circle
  5. One by one share your insights with the team

5 Check out

And finally it’s time to check out

  1. Gather the team standing up in a circle
  2. Ask the question; “How did this session make you feel?”
  3. One by one answer the question
  4. When everyone has answered you're officially checked-out
