Exciting! You and your team are now about to start a 1,5h feedback session, probably a bit daunting as well, so remember that the purpose for doing this is to facilitate clear and open conversations, strengthen the relationships and generate development for your team.

Some things you need before starting:

  • Paper, pens and post-its
  • A empty wall or whiteboard
  • Understand that you and your team are on a learning curve
  • An open minset, what happens during the session is meant to happen

One friendly reminder before we start; it's important that everyone's full attention is turned to the team so take a the last minutes before starting send that urgent email or slack message, and turn your phones on silent mode.
